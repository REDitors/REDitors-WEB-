Pricing Questions

Everything you need to know about our pricing

How do transcription minutes work?
One minute of transcription equals one minute of audio in your video. For example, a 30-minute video uses 30 transcription minutes.
What happens if I run out of minutes?
You can purchase additional minute bundles anytime at $0.10/minute. Professional and Business plans have rollover features.
Can I switch plans anytime?
Yes! Upgrades take effect immediately with prorated billing. Downgrades take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.
Is there a free trial?
Yes! All new users get 120 free minutes. No credit card required. Professional and Business plans include a 14-day trial.
