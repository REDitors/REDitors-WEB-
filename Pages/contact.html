FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How accurate is the transcription?
Our AI achieves 99.5% accuracy on clear audio with minimal background noise. Accuracy may vary based on audio quality, accents, and technical terminology.
What languages are supported?
We support over 50 languages including English, Spanish, French, German, Chinese, Japanese, Arabic, and more. Automatic language detection is included.
How long does transcription take?
Transcription is faster than real-time. A 1-hour video typically takes less than 2 minutes to process.
Is my data secure?
Yes. All audio is encrypted in transit and at rest. We are SOC 2 compliant and never store your audio files after transcription.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
Absolutely. You can cancel anytime from your account settings. You'll retain access until the end of your billing period.
Do you offer refunds?
We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all paid plans. If you're not satisfied, contact us for a full refund.
